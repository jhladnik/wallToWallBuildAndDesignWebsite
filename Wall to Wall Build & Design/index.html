Intro

At Wall to Wall Build and Design, we take pride in our craftsmanship. If you can think it up, there is a great chance we've done it or can do it. We are a design/build firm that can build you brand new as well as well as remodel that 19th century gem that you take so much pride in.

Work

Our scope of work depends on what you are wanting to do in your space. We have the capacities to start from scratch to make a space completely you or if you like the character that the space already has, we can work with the elements that make the space unique. We have completely torn out city loft spaces as well as renovated homes from the mid-1800's while keeping the charm that has made the space.

For the individual that would rather have the home custom to the way they live and the way they want the space to look, we do have the ability and expertise to do complete tear-downs and start from the ground up.

About

Wall to Wall has been a family owned-business for over 40 years in the tri-state area. We have generations of experience and have both watched and taken part in the changing of the landscape in the area. We love what we do which is why all of our clients love what we do as well.
